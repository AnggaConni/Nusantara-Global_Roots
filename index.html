What We Are Doing

"Restoring Earth’s Balance, from Ocean to Roots."

Everything is connected. At Nusantara - Global Roots, we believe that restoring the Earth to its natural state requires a profound return to our roots. Intangible Cultural Heritage (ICH) is the bedrock of community survival, holding generations of wisdom on how to live in harmony with nature.

Led by global development experts and international partners, we connect the local to the global. By integrating traditional knowledge with modern science, Ocean Literacy, and robust Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) strategies, we empower communities to protect their heritage and their environment simultaneously.

Our Vision

"A world where humanity and nature thrive in harmony, deeply rooted in cultural heritage and empowered by global education, ocean literacy, and resilience to safeguard the Earth for future generations."

Our Mission

Empowering Communities, Preserving Ecosystems.

  • Education & Cultural Heritage

    To safeguard Intangible Cultural Heritage (ICH) and integrate traditional ecological knowledge into modern educational frameworks.

  • Ocean Literacy & Nature

    To advance Ocean Literacy, bridging the gap between coastal communities and global marine conservation efforts.

  • Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR)

    To build community resilience by developing nature-based DRR strategies that protect both lives and natural habitats.

Strategic Focus

Our Mandates

Operating at the intersection of international standards and grassroots action, our mandates are designed to create lasting, scalable impact.

Global Knowledge Integration

Synthesizing local wisdom (ICH) with global scientific frameworks to develop comprehensive environmental policies and educational curricula.

Capacity Building & Education

Empowering youth and community leaders through specialized training in Ocean Literacy, sustainability, and cultural preservation.

Systemic Resilience (DRR)

Designing and advocating for nature-based solutions and early warning systems to mitigate the impact of climate-induced disasters.

Multilateral Partnerships

Acting as a strategic bridge between grassroots communities, international development agencies, and global conservation frameworks.

Founded by international development professionals. We leverage a global network of partners to bring world-class standards in heritage safeguarding and climate resilience to the local level.
